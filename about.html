About EditKit

EditKit is a collection of browser-based tools designed to streamline video editing workflows. Built for editors who need quick, reliable utilities without the overhead of complex software. This tool will remain free and open-source forever, as I'm an editor who faced similar hassles myself.

What's Offered

  • LUT Previewer: Test color grades instantly before committing to your edit
  • Subtitle Tools: Create, merge, and sync SRT files with precision
  • Project Manager: Calculate timelines and track deliverables efficiently

Philosophy

I believe tools should be fast, focused, and accessible. Everything runs in your browser—no installations, no sign-ups, no data sent to servers. Your files stay on your machine.

Open Source

EditKit is built with web standards and open-source technologies. Feel free to explore the code, suggest features, or contribute improvements.
